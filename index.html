Timeline of Jim's History

Creation of Jim

One night, Emmettdaster, feeling both tired and inspired, decided to create something on MS Paint. In just 15 seconds, he sketched a random figure and named it Jim. Little did Emmett know that this seemingly insignificant creation would soon become an iconic character.

Discovery by Kaden

Initially, Jim sat on the desktop of the server, unnoticed, until Kaden discovered him and introduced him to the rest of the group.

Growing Popularity

Over time, Jim gained some popularity, and the group started sharing his image as a JPEG file in the group chat. As Jim's fame grew, so did the number of variants created.

Dedicated Discord Server

Eventually, the group decided to create a dedicated Discord server to celebrate Jim's legacy. Jim himself even acquired a Discord account, cementing his presence within the community.

Continuing Legacy

Today, Jim continues to thrive within the server, bringing joy to those who know him. His legacy serves as a testament to the power of creativity and the lasting impact of a simple character born on a late-night MS Paint session.
